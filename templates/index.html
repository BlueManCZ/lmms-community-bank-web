All about the community

LMMS Community Bank project was born by the wish of a vast community to add a personal touch to the LMMS.

Even you can contribute

Our group is filling by music composers, producers and developers willing to help you to improve presets or samples you want to share.

You can find us on Discord

We have created our Discord server so we can have a place to discuss our ideas and share LMMS presets and samples.

All presets and samples in one

We plan to release this project as one big library. This file will be possible to import to the LMMS and start using immediately.

Our Discord server

LMMS Community Bank

We use this server as a sharing platform for our presets and samples.

There are channels for discussions about content quality, presets and samples creation and project improvements.

Our members come from all over the world. So don't worry if English is not your native language. We are all musicians, and we understand each other.

You can help us by spreading this server between your friends and over the internet.
